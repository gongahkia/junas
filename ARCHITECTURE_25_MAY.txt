==================================

(frozen, compat-only). The live "classifier/sorter" is the deterministic
engine below.

Key things this diagram makes obvious
-------------------------------------

• The deterministic engine IS the classifier. /classify is just a legacy
  compatibility shim now.

• The LLM tier is a SIDE BRANCH, not the main path — only fires under
  audit_grade + ambiguous-score band + tenant opt-in.

• Everything privacy-sensitive (LLM, retrieval) passes through PrivacyGuard
  before any external call.

• Three persistence stores have different durability semantics:
    - journal: append-only HMAC-chained
    - mapping store: mutable, Fernet-encrypt opt-in
    - session/matter store: mutable-by-design for inheritance

• The build-time fixture pipeline feeds the recall/precision locks that
  gate every detector and every trained artefact.
