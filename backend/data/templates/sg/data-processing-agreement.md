# DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT

**Date:** {{date}}

**Parties:** {{controller}} ("Organisation") and {{intermediary}} ("Data Intermediary").

## 1. SERVICES
The Data Intermediary processes personal data only for {{services}} and only on documented instructions from the Organisation.

## 2. PDPA OBLIGATIONS
The Data Intermediary shall implement reasonable security arrangements and retention controls for personal data processed on behalf of the Organisation.

## 3. SUB-PROCESSING
The Data Intermediary must not appoint a sub-processor without prior written approval from the Organisation.

## 4. INCIDENT NOTICE
The Data Intermediary shall notify the Organisation without undue delay after becoming aware of a suspected personal data breach.

## 5. RETURN OR DELETION
At termination, the Data Intermediary shall return or securely delete personal data unless retention is required by Singapore law.

## 6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of Singapore.
