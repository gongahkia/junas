# SERVICE AGREEMENT

**Date:** {{date}}

**Parties:** {{supplier}} ("Supplier") and {{customer}} ("Customer").

## 1. SERVICES
The Supplier shall provide {{services}} in accordance with agreed statements of work.

## 2. FEES AND PAYMENT
The Customer shall pay {{fees}} according to valid invoices. Late payments may be suspended after written notice.

## 3. DELIVERABLES
The Supplier shall correct material defects in deliverables notified within a reasonable acceptance period.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party shall protect the other party's confidential information and use it only for this Agreement.

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Pre-existing intellectual property remains with its owner. Project deliverables transfer to the Customer only to the extent stated in a signed statement of work.

## 6. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTES
This Agreement is governed by Singapore law. The parties shall first attempt good-faith negotiation before litigation or arbitration in Singapore.
