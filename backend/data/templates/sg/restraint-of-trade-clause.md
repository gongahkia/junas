# RESTRAINT OF TRADE CLAUSE

## 1. PARTIES
This clause applies between {{employer}} and {{employee}}.

## 2. LEGITIMATE INTEREST
The parties acknowledge that the Employer seeks to protect confidential information, customer connections, and workforce stability.

## 3. RESTRICTED ACTIVITY
For {{duration}} after employment ends, the Employee shall not solicit customers with whom the Employee had material dealings in relation to {{restricted_business}} within {{territory}}.

## 4. LIMITS
This clause does not prevent the Employee from holding passive investments, working in unrelated roles, or using general skill and experience.

## 5. SEVERANCE
If a restriction is unenforceable, the parties intend the unenforceable part to be severed only to the extent permitted by Singapore law.
