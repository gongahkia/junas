# INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

**Date:** {{date}}

**Parties:** {{client}} ("Client") and {{contractor}} ("Contractor").

## 1. SERVICES
The Contractor shall provide {{services}} with reasonable skill and care.

## 2. FEES
The Client shall pay {{fee}} according to invoices properly issued by the Contractor.

## 3. INDEPENDENT STATUS
The Contractor is an independent contractor and is not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of the Client.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Contractor shall keep the Client's confidential information confidential during and after the engagement.

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Unless otherwise agreed in writing, deliverables created specifically for the Client are assigned to the Client upon full payment.

## 6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of Singapore.
